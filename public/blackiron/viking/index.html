Viking Forge is located in at Barnyard Shopping Village in Carmel California. The Viking Forge is located near Carmel Mission (Mission San Carlos Borroméo de Carmelo) and just few minutes drive from downtown Carmel. Stop by the shop and see black iron transformed into fine art.
