Our Tourist Photographs, a Sampling
    Carmel (officially Carmel-By-The-Sea) is filled with many art galleries, excellent restaurants, and fun shops. Add to that, a most beautiful setting, wonderful beach, and great sunsets. Remember to bring coats and sweaters, the coastal fog can sneak in unannounced, bring cash (some excellent smaller breakfast-lunch restaurants don’t take plastic) and above all, think reservations.
