 The Beach Chalet


 
Beach Chalet was built in 1925. The lower level houses the Golden Gate Park Visitor Center and some wonderful depression era murals commissioned by the Work Progress Administration (WPA) in 1936. The upper level houses the new Beach Chalet Brewery and Restaurant.
