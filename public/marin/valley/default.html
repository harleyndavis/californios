Valley Memorial Park
Novato, Marin County, California
     Valley Memorial Park is a modern, traditional cemetery. This cemetery opened in 1960 and is well maintained. It has had a few name changes sine 1960. Visit the Valley Memorial Park website.

Valley Memorial Park Location
650 Bugeia Lane, Novato, CA 94945 From Hwy. 101, take Atherton Ave. east 0.9 miles, then turn northwardly onto Bugeia Lane. Entrance to cemetery is .03 miles from Atherton Ave.
