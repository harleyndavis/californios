The Sausalito News
January 1, 1892, 3:1

LOCAL ITEMS
...

   The funeral of Catherine Domergue, whose death notice apperaed in our issue of this date, took place from the family residence in Sausalito last Sunday. The last funeral rites were conducted by the very Rev. Father Valentine at St. Mary's Cathedral, Sausalito. After a very impressive service by the Rev. gentleman, the funeral cortege moved to Sausalito New Cemetery, this being the first interment in the new burial ground. Out of respect to a brother Forester, Court Star of California, Sausalito, No. 7813 of the Ancient Order of Foresters, the following brothers acted as pall-bearers: Charles Burrey, D.H.G.R.: Paul Trouette, J.E. Creed, Judge Simpton, R.S.; Ed Wosser, S.W.; Fred Lindsey, several members of the Order joining in solemn procession. Mrs. Domergue had been a great sufferer for twelve months. "Her end was peace."

The Sausalito News
January 1, 1892, 3:3
DOMERGUE--In Sausalito, December 15, Cartherine, beloved wife of Gaston Domergue and mother of Wilhelmina Gaston and John Domergue, a native of Buffalo, N.Y., age 48 years.
