The Sausalito News
May 5, 1893, 3:2

LOCAL ITEMS
...

   The law requiring the closing of the old Sausalito Cemetery, parties wishing bodies of deceased friends and relatives removed to the new Sausalito Cemetery can receive attention by applying to Undertaker and Coroner E. Eden San Rafael.

----o----

Notes:
1. "Old Sausalito Cemetery" aka "Sunny Hill Cemetery" (site), Rodeo Ave. near Alta Ave.
2. "New Sausalito Cemetery" is now Forever Fernwood.
