The Sausalito News
June 25, 1885, 3:2

LOCAL NEWS
---o---

   The Cemetery Committee appointed at the citizens meeting met at the "El Monte" Hotel, on Friday evening last. There were present Messrs. Sinkly, Reed, Furlong, Lawrence and Loriano.
   The object of the meeting was to determine upon a course of action with reference to the propositions made by the Land Company asking for a guaranty. It was agreed in Committee that the members should canvass their neighborhoods to learn the individual feelings of citizens giving a pledge to buy lots in the proposed tract.

----o----

Note:
1. Sausalito Land and Ferry Company.
