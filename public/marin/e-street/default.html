E Street Cemetery Site
at St. Paul's Protestant Episcopal Church
San Rafael, Marin County, California


Map: 1906

     Located just south of St Paul's Protestant Episcopal Church on E Street, San Rafael, this cemetery was discontinued and the remains of all persons interred in the cemetery were moved to either Mt. Tamalpais Cemetery or to Mt. Olivet Cemetery. The last remains were removed in 1927.
