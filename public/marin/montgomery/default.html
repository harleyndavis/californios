Montgomery Memorial Chapel
San Anselmo, Marin County, California
San Francisco Theological Seminary
An Institution of the Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.)

     The Montgomery Chapel contains the mortal remains of Alexander Montgomery. He was a major patron of the San Francisco Theological Seminary during the late eighteen hundreds. Alexander Montgomery died in 1893. Because the chapel was not finished at that time, his remains were interred at Laurel Hill Cemetery, San Francisco. Only during 1897 after the chapel was completed, were his remains transfered to the vault in the chapel.
     The Chapel is connected by a very short Loggia (breezeway) to Montague Hall. The beautiful Montgomery Chapel and Montague Hall may be rented for weddings, etc. The chapel is located at the corner of Richmond Road and Bolinas Avenue in San Anselmo, California.
