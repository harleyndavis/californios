Olema Cemetery
Olema, Marin County, California

     Olema Cemetery, Hwy. 1, Olema, California. This cemetery is hidden in plain sight. Cemetery is set back from the road, it is easy to drive past the cemetery without seeing it. It is located about 1 mile south of the junction of Hwy. 1 and Sir Francis Drake Boulevard, on the east side of Hwy.
