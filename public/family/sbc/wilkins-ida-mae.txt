Ida Mae Wilkins
Mother of Viola Wilkins Lossman and Margarite Wilkins. 
Viola Wilkins Lossman is the second wife John Arnold Lossman.
John Arnold Lossman, his first wife, Ethel Mae Smoot Lossman, 
Viola Wilkins Lossman, and Margarite Wilkins are all interred
at the Cherokee Memorial Park, Lodi, San Joaquin County, CA.
