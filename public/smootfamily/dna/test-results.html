    Thomas Middleton Smoot, b.c. 1790 Culpeper VA ?
    Thomas Middleton Smoot born by 1790 first appears in Culpeper County, Virginia, moving to Warren County Tennessee in the 1820s, there he died in 1846. His two sons, Daniel Smoot, born 1814, and William, born 1816 accompanied Thomas M. Smoot in his move. Later Daniel Smoot moved to Lynchburg, Lincoln County, Tennessee (now Moore County, TN). Daniel had eleven children, including five sons. William Smoot remained in Warren County where he had ten children, including six sons.
    DNA donor (Kit 40379) is descendant of William Smoot (of VA & TN) through William’s son Isaac Anderson Smoot.
