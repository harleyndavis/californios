Peter Shanholtzer to John Smoot
A Bill of Sale ~ 1799
(Hampshire County Virginia Deed Book 12, page 122)
Transcription contributed by Dennis M. Smoot

     This Indenture made this ninth day of June in the year of our Lord one thousand seven hundred and ninety nine between Peter Shinhold of the County of Hampshire and State of Virginia of the one part and John Smoot of the county and state aforesaid of the other part. Witnesseth that the said Peter Shinhold in order to secure payment of the following debt, to wit, the sum of seventeen pounds due to George Cooper and payable the fifteenth day of next March for which the said John Smoot is jointly bound in a bond with the said Shinhold, in consideration whereof and for the further sum of one dollar in hand paid to me by the said John Smoot, I have bargained and sold and by these presents do bargain and sell and deliver to the said John Smoot as his proper right and property, one three year old yellow bay horse, one wagon and two cows, To have and to hold the above described property, yet nevertheless, if the said Peter Shinhold, his heirs, executors or administrators shall well and truly pay to the said George Cooper the above mentioned sum of seventeen pounds, and to the said John Smoot the same, of one dollar, on or before the fifteenth of next March, then the said property, or which shall be alive of them, shall be returned by the said Smoot to the said Shinhold as if no such sale had been made, but if the said Shinhold should fail to pay the above debts with all costs and interest and charges that may accrue thereon, then the said John Smoot may proceed to sell the same to pay the debts, having first advertised the same in the Winchester newspaper, and out of money arising from said sale to pay the debts, costs, interest and charges and the residue if any to pay to the said Shinhold or his agent if any he have, if not to pay it to anyone lawfully claiming the same. In witness whereof, I Peter Shinhold have hereunto set my hand and affixed my seal this 27th day of June 1799.
Peter Shanholtzer

Teste James Ellanrod     Jas Carruthers

At a court held for Hampshire County the 11th day of December 1799.
     This Bill of Sale from Peter Shanholtzer to John Smoot was proved by the oath of James Carruthers, one of the witnesses thereto, and ordered to be recorded.

And Wodrow
