Edward Smoot to Eleven Smoot
A Deed ~ 1824
Fauquier County, Virginia Deed Book 27, page 427
Transcription contributed by Dennis M. Smoot
Annotated by Dennis M. Smoot
See Smoots of Maryland and Virginia, Edward Smoot6, pp 88-89.

Edward Smoot
to                  | Deed
Eleven Smoot
       I Edward Smoot of the County of Fauquier and State of Virginia for the natural love and affection which I bear toward my son Eleven Smoot, his heirs, executors, administrators or assigns, I do give unto the said Eleven Smoot or his heirs one Negro boy named Alexander to him the said Eleven Smoot, his heirs, executors, administrators or assigns, free from the claims of all and every person whoever. In testimony whereof I have hereunto set my hand and affixed my seal the seventeenth day of January 1824.
Edward Smoot
Witnesses present
John Neale
Josiah Smoot
Edward Smoot Junr

Clerk’s Office of Fauquier County Court, the 24th day of May 1824. This Indenture was acknowledged by Edward Smoot and admitted to record.
Teste                         
Jno A.W. Smith C.C.
