Middleton Smoot ~ Hannah R. W. Northcraft
A Marriage Bond ~ 19 January 1826
(Shenandoah County Virginia)
Contributed by Dennis M. Smoot


Note:

The marriage bond was accompanied with a handwritten permission, as follows:

    This is to certify to the Clerk of Shenandoah County Court that I have given my consent to the marriage of my daughter Hannah R. W. Northcraft to Middleton Smoot, as Witnesseth my Hand and Seal this 18th day of January 1826.
Edwar. Northcraft

Test
Jas F. Gaines
Cornelius Gaines
