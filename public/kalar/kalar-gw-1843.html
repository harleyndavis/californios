George Washington Kalar (son of Jacob Kalar Jr 3 and his wife Sarah /Sallie/ Ryan Long)
Born: 3 May 1843, Porterwood, Shaver Fork, Randolph County Virginia.
Married: Sarah Ann Elizabeth Phares, 3 Dec 1863, at the John Phares home, Randolph County WV.
Died: 2 Oct 1893, Kerens, Randolph County WV.
Buried: Israel Cem., Kerens, Randolph County WV.

Sarah Ann Elizabeth Phares (daughter of John M. Phares and Sidney Ann Rennix)
Born: 5 Oct 1847, Randolph County VA.
Died: 9 Apr 1932, Mountain View, Santa Clara County, CA
Buried: IOOF Cem., Santa Cruz, Santa Cruz County, CA
