John H. Long and his wife Susanna Hull

John H. Long (son of George H. Long and Sarah /Sallie/ Ryan)
     (Pedigree of John H. Long)
Born: 1798, Virginia.
Married: Susanna /Susan/ Hull, 20 Nov 1817, Pendleton County, VA.
Died: May 1838, Randolph County, VA.
Buried: Randolph County VA (now Tucker County WV).

Susanna /Susan/ Hull (daughter of Adam Hull and Esther Keister)
Born: 1798, Crabbottom, Pendleton County, VA.
Died: 28 August 1865. Tucker County WV
Buried: Tucker County WV.
