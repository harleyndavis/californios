Jo Mora’s “The Greeting” (two Californios figures, bowing and curtseying) is found in the tiny courtyard of the privately owned El Paseo building in Carmel, California. Look for the Little Napoli restaurant, located on Dolores Street between Ocean Avenue and 7th and you will find the El Paseo building. (El Paseo: The Passage Way Where New California Meets Old Spain.)
